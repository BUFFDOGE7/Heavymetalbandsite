THE ORIGIN

☠

The band did not form in any normal way, and none of its members fully agree on how it even happened. The most widely accepted version begins with a completely doomed cargo flight carrying crates of suspiciously glowing vodka, industrial gardening equipment, decorative cavalry sabers, and thousands of cheaply made festival hats that were never meant to survive transport.


Due to pilot confusion, a broken GPS, and someone allegedly replacing aviation fuel with energy drink, the aircraft veered wildly off course and crashed directly into the main stage of a small but very loud heavy metal festival — during a thunderstorm.

At the exact moment of impact, lightning struck the wreckage and surged through a half-broken amplifier. The amp exploded into life, producing a distorted noise so aggressive that several nearby speakers gave up immediately.


Out of the wreckage crawled five very angry individuals, each holding something completely unrelated to music, each shouting in their own language. Their shouting accidentally synchronized into a perfectly timed metal breakdown.


"This is the worst thing I have ever heard… we need to put this on stage immediately."— Nearby sound engineer. Last known words of sanity.
